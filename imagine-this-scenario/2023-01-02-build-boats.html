Build Boats

Our Main Street is populatred with shops that cater to toursists and those who want, and can afford, luxury goods. While my natural impulse is to speak out against this trivialization of our valuable commercial resources, what if we could figure out a way to stop fighting and start building, by working together to help everyone get what they want?
