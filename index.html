About

beaconny.net is maintained by members of the Beacon Computing Club as a place for local digital content and platform for web technology education and experimentation.

Forums

  • Computing Club

Our discussion forums, which are more like standalone social media sites, are powered by the free and open-source Forem application.
If you'd like to start your own forum, let us know at: forums@beaconny.net

  • We'll provide a free subdomain: your-forum-name.beaconny.net
  • You'll host the application in your own DigitalOcean account (which we'll help you set up) at a cost of about $15/month.
  • No support is provided, but you can ask for help in the great community over at the official Forem site, or in our Computing Club.

Alternatively, Forem offers their own cloud-hosted version that takes care of all the technical details and includes support, and we can give you the subdomain.
